# Story
  - You are son of very rich nobleman. One day, Mr.X and his gang, who aim for 
	your parent fortune, kidnap you to their underground base. Fortunately,
	you are very educated and skilled with rope to fix your immobile state.
	In order to keep your parent fortune safe, you have to fight toward
	the ground and escape Mr.X.  

# Objective
  - You need to look for stair to escape to above the ground(to the next level)
  - At last level, you have to win against Mr.X to end the game.
